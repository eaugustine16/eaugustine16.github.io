Be Rewarded for Being You

Gleebie brings you personalized promotions from your favorite retailers on items you really want. Better yet, you get them when you're right in front of the store.

Don't waste your time with deals you don't want. Make sure everything fits just right. Avoid the hassle of returning items ordered online that just didn't meet your expectations.

Learn more

Step 1: Sign-up!

Don't do anything out of the ordinary! Simply sign into Gleebie with your email, facebook, and or phone number and browse online stores like you normally would. Retailers can reach out through our app to you with personalized promotions when you're in front of their stores.

Step 2: Scan Your Promo Code

Gleebie makes getting deals simple. No sending pictures of receipts, no need to redeem points for gift cards. Just scan your promotion's code in the Gleebie app at the checkout counter and your discount will be processed right there!

Step 3: Get Personalized Deals

Don't do all the work hunting for deals. No more coupon cutting. Make yourself a VIP and have deals come to you. The best part? No unwanted deals cluttering your mailbox. The promotions are curated for you based on what you have already expressed interest in online.
